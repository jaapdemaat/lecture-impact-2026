Impact Category Definitions
TRANSFORMATIVE (5pts): Fundamentally changed student perspective — "eye-opening", "shifted how I see"
INTEGRATIVE (4pts): Actionable insight gained — "helped me understand", "applies to practice"
RESONANT (3pts): Personal relevance, affirmed thinking — "resonated", "inspiring", "compelling"
CRITICAL (3pts): Productive challenge, critical reflection — "questioned", "forced me to think"
MENTIONED (1pt): Cited without elaborating on impact — "watched", "attended", "discussed"
